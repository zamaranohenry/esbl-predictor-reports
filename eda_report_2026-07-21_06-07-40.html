2. Class Distribution (Target Variable)

The full study cohort contains 1,569 patients. Of the 660 patients with a confirmed ESBL result, 281 (42.6%) are ESBL-positive and 379 (57.4%) are ESBL-negative. The remaining 909 patients had no bacterial isolate cultured ('Not applicable') and therefore have no ESBL result — they are retained in the EDA cohort but will be excluded from model training. Among labelled patients, the class distribution is near-balanced (42.6% positive), which is favourable for binary classification.
